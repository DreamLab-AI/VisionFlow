## Security Token

Security Token refers to a token designed to represent complete or fractional ownership interests in assets and/or entities. subject to restrictions based on identity, jurisdiction, and asset category, and regulated by securities laws providing investors with entitlements like dividends, profit-sharing, or voting privileges.
