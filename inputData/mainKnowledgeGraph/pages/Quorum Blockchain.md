## Quorum Blockchain

Quorum Blockchain refers to a component of the blockchain ecosystem.

- Quorum is widely adopted in industries requiring secure, permissioned blockchain networks, particularly in finance, supply chain, and healthcare sectors.
  - Notable organisations leveraging Quorum include major financial institutions and consortia seeking private blockchain solutions with Ethereum compatibility.
  - In the UK, financial hubs such as London have seen adoption of Quorum-based solutions, while innovation centres in North England cities like Manchester and Leeds are increasingly exploring Quorum for supply chain transparency and digital identity projects.
- Technically, Quorum supports high transaction throughput with enhanced privacy features such as private smart contracts and permissioned access controls.
  - Limitations remain around interoperability with public blockchains and the complexity of deploying and managing permissioned networks at scale.
- Quorum aligns with emerging blockchain standards and frameworks promoting enterprise interoperability, data privacy, and regulatory compliance.
