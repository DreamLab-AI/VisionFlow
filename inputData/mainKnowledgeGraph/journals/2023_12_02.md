	- The finance & insurance sector has the highest exposure to AI out of all industries. Other highly exposed sectors are information & communication, professional/scientific/technical services, property, public administration, and [[Education and AI]].

	- Employees with higher qualification levels, like university degrees, tend to work in jobs with greater AI exposure compared to lower qualified employees.
	- Subjects like accounting, finance, economics, and mathematics lead to the most AI-exposed jobs, while lower-level qualifications in manual trades have the least exposure.
