- [[Nerfs]]
  [NeRF: Neural Radiance Fields](https://www.matthewtancik.com/nerf) by Matthew Tancik presents a method for synthesizing novel views of complex scenes using a deep network. This technique supports detailed scene geometry representation and mixed-reality applications. The follow-up project, mip-NeRF, further enhances detail representation and reduces aliasing artifacts.
